Computer Aid partners with Paylink Solutions

Computer Aid has formed a partnership with Paylink Solutions, a fintech company, to support charitable endeavors. Paylink Solutions recently completed a bikeathon fundraiser and plans to continue raising money through events like a half marathon and a Christmas market. The funds raised will go towards creating Solar Community Hubs and providing access to IT resources and learning opportunities for those in need. Computer Aid appreciates Paylink Solutions' efforts and looks forward to a long-term collaboration, with the shared goal of making a positive impact through technology. Donations can be made through JustGiving.

Utelize Mobile raises more than £120,000 for Computer Aid to fund a life-changing tech hub in South Africa

A partnership between Utelize Mobile, Dell Technologies, and Computer Aid International has brought digital access to rural communities in South Africa. Utelize Mobile raised £120,000 by reselling unwanted corporate smartphones, funding a solar-powered digital center in Xitlhelani, Limpopo. This initiative aims to bridge the digital divide, providing internet access, computers, and education. The Solar Community Hub program, in partnership with Dell Technologies, offers tailored services to meet community needs, including technology access, healthcare, and workforce skills. Since 1997, Computer Aid has helped over 14.5 million people worldwide, providing over 260,000 computers in more than 100 countries, enabling at least 1 billion hours of learning.
